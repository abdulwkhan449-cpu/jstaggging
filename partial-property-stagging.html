Our Services

Partial Property Staging

Enhance your property's appeal without a full overhaul. Our partial staging service works with your existing furniture and adds carefully selected pieces to create a polished, inviting look.

What's Included

WHAT'S INCLUDED IN A PARTIAL PROPERTY JS STAGING PACKAGE?

Initial Consultation – This is the first step in transforming your home into a beautifully styled, market-ready property. This on-site visit allows us to assess your space, understand your goals, and create a tailored staging plan that maximizes buyer appeal.
Furniture & Décor Selection – our team will carefully select our most suited furniture, artwork, rugs, and accessories to enhance your home's best features.
Professional Installation – Our team delivers, arranges, and styles the property to perfection.
Staging for Up to 6 Weeks – Your home remains beautifully staged for the duration of your campaign, with options to extend if needed.
Seamless Removal – Once your property is sold, we handle the collection of all furnishings and decor hassle-free.

A full property staging package ensures your home is presented at its absolute best, helping you sell faster and for a higher price.
